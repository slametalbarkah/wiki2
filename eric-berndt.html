Eric Berndt

Eric Berndt (born April 23, 1979) is an American professional stock car racing driver who competes part-time in the NASCAR Whelen Modified Tour, driving the No. 11 for Ferguson Racing. Nicknamed "The Bulldog", Berndt has spent the majority of his career driving SK Modifieds at Stafford Motor Speedway, Thompson Speedway Motorsports Park, and the Waterford Speedbowl, winning races at all 3 facilities as well as the 2003 track championship at Thompson. Berndt's grandfather Ron has owned racecars in Connecticut dating back to the 1960's.

Berndt has also competed in the Tri-Track Open Modified Series and the Modified Racing Series.
