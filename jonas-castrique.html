Jonas Castrique

Jonas Castrique (born 13 January 1997) is a Belgian professional racing cyclist, who currently rides for UCI ProTeam Wagner Bazin WB. In October 2020, he rode in the 2020 Gent–Wevelgem race in Belgium.
