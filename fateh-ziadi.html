Fateh Ziadi

Fateh Ziadi (born (1976-10-26)October 26, 1976) is an Algerian sport shooter who competes in the men's 10 metre air pistol. At the 2012 Summer Olympics, he finished 43rd in the qualifying round, failing to make the cut for the final.
