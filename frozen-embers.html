Frozen Embers

Frozen Embers is an EP released in 2003 by the American dark wave band the Crüxshadows. It contains tracks from the subsequent full length release, Ethernaut, remixes of tracks from Wishfire, and new tracks. The EP peaked at #2 on the German Alternative Charts (DAC) and ranked #24 on the DAC Top 100 Singles chart for 2003.
