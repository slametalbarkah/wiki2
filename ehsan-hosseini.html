Ehsan Hosseini

Seyed Mohammad Ehsan Hosseini (Persian: سید محمد احسان حسینی born October 3, 1998) is an Iranian footballer who plays as a defender for Nassaji Mazandaran in the Persian Gulf Pro League.





== Club career ==





=== Persepolis F.C. ===

Hosseini joined Persepolis in summer 2017 with a contract until 2020.





== Career statistics ==

As of 25 July 2021





== Honours ==

Persepolis

Iranian Super Cup: 2018, 2019

Hazfi Cup: 2018–19

AFC Champions League runner-up: 2018, 2020
