Didarul Alam

Didarul Alam (Bengali: দিদারুল আলম; born 6 April 1968) is a Bangladeshi businessman and politician and the former member of parliament for Chittagong-4 and director of Mostafa-Hakim Group.





== Early life ==

Alam was born on 6 April 1968. He has a B.A. degree.





== Career ==

Alam was elected to parliament on 5 January 2014 for Chittagong-4 as a Bangladesh Awami League candidate. His supporter and Jubo League leader, Ramzan Ali, was assassinated in August 2018 in Chittagong. Ramzan was accused in a number of criminal cases.
