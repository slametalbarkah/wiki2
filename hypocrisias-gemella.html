Hypocrisias gemella

Hypocrisias is a genus of moths in the family Erebidae erected by George Hampson in 1901.

Hypocrisias lua (Dyar, 1910)

Hypocrisias minima (Neumoegen, 1883)

Hypocrisias punctatus (Druce, 1884)
