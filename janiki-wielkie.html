Janiki Wielkie

Janiki Wielkie [jaˈniki ˈvjɛlkʲɛ] (German Groß Hanswalde) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Zalewo, within Iława County, Warmian-Masurian Voivodeship, in northern Poland.
