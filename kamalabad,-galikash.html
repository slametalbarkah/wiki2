Kamalabad, Galikash

Kamalabad (Persian: كمال اباد, also Romanized as Kamālābād) is a village in Yanqaq Rural District in the Central District of Galikash County, Golestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 1,036, in 231 families.
