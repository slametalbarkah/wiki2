Friendly Beaches

Friendly Beaches is a rural locality in the local government area of Glamorgan–Spring Bay in the South-east region of Tasmania. The locality is about 91 kilometres (57 mi) north-east of the town of Swansea. The 2016 census has a population of 10 for the state suburb of Friendly Beaches.





== History ==

Friendly Beaches is a confirmed suburb/locality.





== Geography ==

The eastern boundary of Friendly Beaches is the Tasman Sea. The Friendly Beaches Reserve is within the locality.





== Road infrastructure ==

The C302 route (Coles Bay Road) enters from the north-west and runs through to the south-west, where it exits.
