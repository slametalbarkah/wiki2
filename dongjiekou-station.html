Dongjiekou station

Dongjiekou Station (Chinese: 东街口站; pinyin: Dōngjiēkǒu zhàn; Fuzhounese: [tøŋ˨˩ ɛ˥˧ au˧˧]) is a metro station of Line 1 and Line 4 of the Fuzhou Metro. This station is located at the intersection of Bayiqi North Road and Yangqiao East Road in Gulou District, Fuzhou, Fujian, China. Service at this station began on January 6, 2017. The Three Lanes and Seven Alleys is located southwest of this station.





== Station layout ==

Dongjiekou station has a round concourse on B1 floor, below which is the platform for Line 1. This station also has another level for Line 4.
