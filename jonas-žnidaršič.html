Jonas Žnidaršič

Jonas Žnidaršič (born 30 January 1962 in Novo Mesto) is a Slovenian television personality and journalist. He is best known for hosting the Slovenian version of Who Wants to Be a Millionaire. He has appeared on Late Night Poker in Great Britain.
