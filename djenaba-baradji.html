Djenaba Baradji

Djenaba Baradji (born 16 December 1995) is a footballer who plays as a midfielder for the Mali women's national team. Born in France, she represented Mali at international level.





== International career ==

Baradji competed for Mali at the 2018 Africa Women Cup of Nations, playing in two matches.
