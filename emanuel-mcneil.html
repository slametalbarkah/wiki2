Emanuel McNeil

Emanuel McNeil (born June 9, 1967) is a former American football defensive tackle. He played for the New England Patriots in 1989, the New York Jets in 1990 and for the Winnipeg Blue Bombers in 1991. He was drafted in the tenth round of the 1989 NFL Draft by the Patriots.
