Jorge Bava

Jorge Rodrigo Bava (born 2 August 1981) is a Uruguayan football manager and former player who played as a goalkeeper. He is the current manager of Colombian club Santa Fe.





== Career ==

In January 2010 Rosario Central signed the Uruguayan goalkeeper from Libertad to a two-year contract.

On 7 January 2017, Bava signed with MLS club Chicago Fire to a one-year contract with two one-year options. His contract was terminated by mutual consent on 18 January 2018.
